Temperatures at Different Latitudes Humidity at Different Latitudes Cloudiness at Different Latitudes Wind Speed at Different Latitudes
This project showcases comparisons of different weather patterns for cities across the globe, specifically at cities' latitudes. The following weather patterns were examined: temperature (in Fahrenheit), humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed (mph). Throughout this site, you can navigate to data visualizations as well as examine the data itself. Enjoy the findings, and we hope you learn some interesting facts about weather trends across the globe!
